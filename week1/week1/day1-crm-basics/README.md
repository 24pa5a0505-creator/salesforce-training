## 🛠 Key Technical Concepts

### Salesforce
Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform used to manage customer relationships, sales, marketing, and support services.

### CRM (Customer Relationship Management)
CRM helps organizations manage customer interactions, track sales activities, and improve customer satisfaction.

### Standard Objects
- Account
- Contact
- Lead
- Opportunity

### Custom Objects
Objects created based on specific business requirements.

### Relationships
- Lookup Relationship
- Master-Detail Relationship
